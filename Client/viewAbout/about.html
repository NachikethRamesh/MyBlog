About

Hi! I am Nachiketh. Thank you for visitng my page.

During the second year of university, I decided to start a blog to post football analysis. While the site still exits, it has been dormant over the last three years. You can find it as EarlyShower on Wordpress.

I also started writing about the general aspects of my life and about the things I found intriguing on two separate blogs for reasons that I don't recall now. If ever I remember why I created two different blogs, I guess I will be baffled by the logical reasoning of my younger self. I guess I was, as the business people say, DIVERSIFYING!

You can find my blogs on Medium and Wordpress.

I wanted to write essays once again. Since I am not ready to buy a plan on Wordpress, I get only a few selected features. I can't customize my page beyond a limit. Because of the restrictions put by Wordpress, I had moved to Medium a couple of years ago. However, Medium is nothing more than a market place of all sorts of pieces and standing out in that crowd is impossible for me. Additionally, I can do no custom edits to my page on Medium. So I thought of building my own blog.

I coded everything from scratch. I have used Express + Node for backend and HTML, CSS, and plain Javascript for front end. I have used Mongo DB to support my back end.

I would be lying if I told that I needed no outside help in coding the HTML, CSS and JS elements of this webpage. I relied heavily on certain Youtube channels and a few articles.

I am new to web development and as I learn more, I will add new features to the page. So as long as you don't find a comment section below the articles, please drop all your comments and thoughts at nrames609@gmail.com
